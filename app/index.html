Hello

Welcome to the website for MD consulting the trading name of Myra Duncan. You may be looking for general management consultancy, marketing consultancy or health and care consultancy, or something you're not quite sure about yet and think you need some help. Whichever it is why not contact me for a chat?

MD Consulting is an independent business management consultancy based on the Isle of Skye, providing services across Scotland and the UK. The site gives a general overview of services, however why not get in touch and we can talk through what you are looking for and the types of consultancy, advice and support md consulting can provide; no obligation either way . To find out more about md consulting click here to go to the 'Who we are' page.
